## Metaphor Structure Template

Typical structure for metaphor-driven pieces:

1. **Hook** - Introduce metaphor through story/scenario
2. **Setup** - Establish the comparison clearly
3. **Mapping** - Show how metaphor elements align with reality
4. **Implications** - Explore what the metaphor reveals
5. **Counterpoint** - Address where metaphor breaks down (builds trust)
6. **Payoff** - Use metaphor to reframe the problem/solution
